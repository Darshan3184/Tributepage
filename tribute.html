Tribute to Ratan Tata

About Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is a renowned Indian industrialist and philanthropist, known for his visionary leadership at the Tata Group, one of India's largest and oldest conglomerates. Born on December 28, 1937, he took over the reins of the Tata Group in 1991 and transformed it into a global powerhouse. Under his leadership, the group expanded into various sectors, including steel, automobiles, IT, and hospitality. Ratan Tata is also known for his commitment to social causes and has been instrumental in various philanthropic initiatives, focusing on education, healthcare, and rural development.
